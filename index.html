Harry Stoltz

I’m a graduate student in mathematics at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, New York University.  My research interests lie in the intersection of mathematics and physics.

I taught my course, "International Relations of Spaceflight," eight times (Quarterly, Winter 2023 through Spring 2025) at the University of California, Riverside.  An up-to-date copy of the syllabus can be found here, along with more information about the course.

I have also written articles on spaceflight for a couple outlets, including Space Explored, CleanTechnica, Inverse, and UCR's student newspaper: The Highlander.

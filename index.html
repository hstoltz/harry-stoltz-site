Harry Stoltz

I’m a graduate student in mathematics at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, New York University.  My research interests lie in the intersection of mathematics and physics. I taught my course, "International Relations of Spaceflight," eight times (Winter 2023, Spring 2023, Fall 2023, Winter 2024, Spring 2024, Fall 2024, Winter 2025, Spring 2025) at the University of California, Riverside.  An up-to-date copy of the syllabus can be found here, along with more information about the course. I have also written articles on spaceflight for a couple outlets, including Space Explored, CleanTechnica, Inverse, and UCR's student newspaper: The Highlander.

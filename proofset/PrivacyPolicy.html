Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Summary: Proofset collects minimal data necessary to provide the service. We process your images to convert them to LaTeX, but we don't store your images on our servers after processing. We never sell your data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Proofset ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Harry Stoltz, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Proofset, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

These practices are part of our broader Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account identification and communication
  • Authentication data - Securely managed by Firebase Authentication (Sign in with Apple or email/password)

If you use Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your email address. In this case, Apple provides us with a private relay email address.

2.2 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the App:

  • Page count - Number of pages processed (to enforce usage limits)
  • Subscription status - Whether you're on Free or Pro tier
  • Account creation date

2.3 Content You Provide

When you use the App, you may provide:

  • Images - Photos of handwritten notes you upload for conversion
  • Documents - The resulting LaTeX code and PDF files

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • General crash and error logs (to improve the App)

3. How We Use Your Information

Information Purpose
Email address Account management, important service updates
Images you upload AI processing to convert to LaTeX (processed in real-time, not stored)
Usage counts Enforce free tier limits, track subscription usage
Subscription status Provide appropriate features and limits
Error logs Diagnose issues and improve the App

4. How We Process Your Images

Important: Your images are processed in real-time and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.

When you upload an image for conversion:

  1. Your image is sent securely (via HTTPS) to our backend server
  2. The image is forwarded to Google's Gemini AI for processing
  3. The AI generates LaTeX code from your handwritten content
  4. The LaTeX is returned to your device
  5. The image is discarded from our servers immediately after processing

Google may process your images according to their Privacy Policy and Data Processing terms for the Gemini API.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Account data - Stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) servers in the United States
  • Documents (LaTeX/PDF) - Stored locally on your device
  • Images - Stored locally on your device; only transmitted temporarily for processing

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Access controls and authentication tokens
  • Regular security reviews

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Export

You can access your documents directly in the App. All your LaTeX and PDF files are stored on your device and can be exported at any time.

8.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will:

  • Delete your account information from our servers
  • Remove your usage history
  • Cancel any active subscription (refunds handled by Apple)

Note: Documents stored locally on your device will not be deleted automatically.

8.3 Email Communications

We only send essential service-related emails. We do not send marketing emails.
